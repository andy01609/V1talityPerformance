Ling Do: Personal Trainer

Hi, I'm Ling Do, founder of Do Fitness. At 13, I fell in love with fitness, captivated by the transformative power of diet and exercise. Over the years, this fascination evolved from performing calisthenics to pursuing bodybuilding in my 20s, all fueled by my unwavering commitment to personal growth and well-being. Discovering the joy of guiding family and friends on their fitness journeys, my hobby naturally transitioned into a career. As a personal trainer, I take great satisfaction in empowering others to achieve their health goals, using a dynamic, creative, and enjoyable approach. At Do Fitness, we're not just about transforming bodies; we're about transforming lifestyles. Fitness is more than a physical state - it's a state of mind, a commitment to embracing healthier habits that last a lifetime. Let's embark on this journey to a healthier, stronger you, together.
